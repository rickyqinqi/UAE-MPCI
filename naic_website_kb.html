概述 Overview

通过或进入阿联酋运输货物的船公司、货运代理和 NVOCC 需要向 NAIC（国家预申报信息中心）提交货物数据。仅适用于集装箱货物，包括进口、中转和 FROB（船上剩余货物）。

Shipping lines, freight forwarders, and NVOCCs transporting cargo through or into the UAE are required to file shipment data with the National Advance Information Center (NAIC). This applies exclusively to containerized cargo, including import, transshipment, and FROB (Freight Remaining on Board).

NAIC uses accredited integration service providers to facilitate the filing process. The following options are available:

  • 船公司：可以选择以下任一方式： / Shipping Line: Can choose to either:

申报范围 Scope of Filing

以下货物类型需要申报：

The following cargo types are required to file:

  • 船公司：必须提交直接提单（非拼箱货物）或主提单（拼箱货物）。 / Shipping Line: Must file either the Direct Bill of Lading (for non-consolidated shipments) or the Master Bill of Lading (for consolidated shipments).
  • 货运代理和 NVOCC：必须提交拼箱货物的分提单。 / Freight Forwarders and NVOCCs: Must file the House Bills of Lading for consolidated shipments.

豁免范围 Exclusions from Filing

以下货物类型和情况免予申报：

The following cargo types and situations are excluded from filing requirements:

  • 出口货物：从阿联酋出口的货物。 / Export Shipments: Shipments being exported from the UAE.
  • 非集装箱海运货物：散装货物（散货、液体散货）、滚装货物等。 / Non-Containerized Maritime Cargo: Bulk cargo (breakbulk, liquid bulk), Ro-Ro shipments, etc.
  • 木质船舶：目前 MPCI 实施阶段的木质船舶除外。 / Wooden Vessels: Excluded for the current phase of MPCI implementation.
  • 空集装箱：内部无货物的集装箱。 / Empty Containers: Containers without cargo inside.
  • 船员物品：船员个人物品。 / Crew's Effects: Personal belongings of the vessel's crew.

申报格式 Filing Format

所有提单必须使用 EDIFACT CUSCAR 格式提交。使用服务提供商时，申报流程的技术方面将由提供商处理。

All BOLs must be submitted in the EDIFACT CUSCAR format. When using a service provider, the technical aspects of the filing process will be handled by the provider.

必填信息 Required Information

主要包含提单上的数据，以及以下附加信息：

Primarily consists of the data found on the Bill of Lading, along with additional information such as:

  • 提单号码 / Bill Number
  • 签发日期和地点 / Issue Date and Location
  • 港口（装载、卸货、中转、收货和交货） / Ports (Loading, Discharge, Transshipment, Receipt, and Delivery)
  • 集装箱详情和封号 / Container Details & Seal Numbers
  • 货物详情和 HS 编码 / Goods Details & HS Code
  • 相关方（发货人、收货人、通知方、货运代理和代理人） / Involved Parties (Shipper, Consignee, Notify Party, Freight Forwarders and Agents)
  • 船舶和航次信息：对船公司强制。 / Vessel & Voyage Information: Mandatory for shipping lines.
  • 标识符：船公司和货运代理必须提供其自有标识符：MPCI Party ID。此外，货运代理必须包含主拼箱方或船公司的标识符。船公司或主拼箱方在拼箱情况下必须包含任何拼箱（子）货运代理的标识符。 / Identifiers: Shipping lines and freight forwarders must provide their own identifiers: MPCI Party ID. Additionally, freight forwarders must include identifiers for the master co-loader or shipping line. Shipping lines or master co-loaders must include identifiers for any consolidating (child) freight forwarders in cases of consolidation.
  • 其他标准信息：通常包含在提单中的任何其他标准信息。 / Other Standard Information: Any additional standard information typically included in the BOL.

MPCI Party ID MPCI Party ID

MPCI Party ID 是 NAIC 分配给每个注册船公司或货运代理的唯一标识符。

The MPCI Party ID is a unique identifier assigned by NAIC to each registered shipping line or freight forwarder.

  • 船公司：在计划启动日期临近时，将提供完整的船公司 MPCI Party ID 列表。 / Shipping Line: A complete list of Shipping Line MPCI Party IDs will be available as we approach the program launch date.
  • 货运代理：必须在需要向船公司或海外货运代理提供自己的 MPCI Party ID 以用于申报。 / Freight Forwarders: Must provide their own MPCI Party ID to shipping line or overseas freight forwarders when necessary for filing purposes.

申报委托 Delegation of Filing

  • 船公司：可以将申报流程委托给其运输代理，但代理在申报时必须提供船公司的 MPCI 代码。责任仍由船公司承担。 / Shipping Line: May delegate the filing process to their shipping agents, but the agent must provide the Shipping Line's MPCI code during filing. The liability remains with the Shipping Line.
  • 本地货运代理/NVOCC：可以委托海外货运代理进行申报，海外货运代理在申报时必须提供本地货运代理的 MPCI 代码。责任仍由本地货运代理承担。 / Local Freight Forwarders/NVOCCs: Can delegate filing to overseas freight forwarders, who must provide the local freight forwarder's MPCI code during filing. The liability remains with the local freight forwarder.

注册要求 Registration Requirements

所有实体（船公司、货运代理、NVOCC 和运输代理）必须在开始申报之前向 NAIC 注册。

All entities (shipping line, freight forwarders, NVOCCs, and shipping agents) must register with NAIC before initiating filings.

Registration roles include:

申报时限 Filing Deadlines

船公司：

Shipping Lines:

  • 如果提单直接签发给发货人（无货运代理拼箱）：必须在进入阿联酋前的最后一个装货港口装货前 24 小时提交。 / If the Bill of Lading is issued directly to the shipper (no consolidation by a freight forwarder): must be submitted 24 hours before loading at the last loading port before the UAE.
  • 如果提单用于拼箱货物：必须在进入阿联酋前的最后一个装货港口装货前 6 小时提交。 / If the Bill of Lading is for a consolidated shipment: must be submitted 6 hours before loading at the last loading port before the UAE.

货运代理或 NVOCC：

Freight Forwarders or NVOCC:

  • 如果分提单包含实际收货人和发货人：必须在进入阿联酋前的最后一个装货港口装货前 24 小时提交。 / If the House Bill of Lading includes the actual consignee and shipper: must be submitted 24 hours before loading.
  • 如果提单合并多个货物（收货人是另一家货运代理）：必须在进入阿联酋前的最后一个装货港口装货前 6 小时提交。 / If the Bill of Lading consolidates multiple shipments (consignee is another freight forwarder): must be submitted 6 hours before loading.

示例 1：货物在中国装货，船只在斯里兰卡停靠，未卸货重装，则最后一个装货港仍为中国，应在装货前 24 小时提交。

Example 1: A shipment is loaded in China and the vessel stops in Sri Lanka without unloading. The last loading port remains China, and filing should be submitted 24 hours before loading.

示例 2：货物在中国装货，在斯里兰卡卸货后再装载到另一艘船前往阿联酋。由于货物在斯里兰卡被物理重新装载，斯里兰卡成为最后一个装货港，应在装货前 24 小时提交。

Example 2: Cargo loaded in China, unloaded in Sri Lanka, then reloaded onto another vessel. Sri Lanka becomes the last loading port, filing should be submitted 24 hours before loading.

注意：时限仅适用于初次申报。更新可以在货物旅程中任何时间进行，甚至到达后也可以。 / Note: Deadlines apply only to the initial filing. Updates can be made at any point during the shipment's journey.

更新与修改 Updates & Corrections

任何提单更新都必须提交给 NAIC。包括但不限于：更正、路线更改、货物拆分、换单、或对 NAIC 信息请求的响应。

Any updates to the Bill of Lading must be submitted to NAIC. This includes, but is not limited to, corrections, route changes, shipment splits, switch bills, or updates in response to an RFI from NAIC.

  • NAIC 将对每次提交（包括修改）发出响应。 / A response will be issued for every filing, including amendments.
  • 收到 ACT 后仍可提交修改。 / Amendments can still be submitted after receiving an ACT response.
  • 装载后提交更新仍可能收到 RFI。 / RFIs may still be issued after loading.
  • 在极少数情况下，装载后更新核心要素可能导致重新评估，甚至产生 DNL。 / Updating core elements after loading may lead to re-assessment, which in rare cases could result in a DNL.
  • 货物不再前往阿联酋（取消或路线变更）时，必须取消原申报。 / A filing must be canceled if the shipment is no longer coming to the UAE.

MPCI 适用于海运货物，而 PLACI（预装前货物信息）适用于空运货物。 / MPCI applies to maritime cargo, whereas PLACI (Pre-Loading Advance Cargo Information) applies to air cargo.

申报要求与责任 | Filing Requirements & Responsibilities

谁负责申报？
Who is responsible for filing?
船公司或其委托的运输代理、以及货运代理和 NVOCC 均负责申报。每个申报方对其签发的提单负责，货运代理对其海外对应方签发的提单负责。 / Filing is required by shipping lines or their delegated shipping agents, as well as freight forwarders and NVOCCs. Each filer is liable for submitting the Bill of Lading they issue.
NAIC 如何定义责任？
How does NAIC define liability?
NAIC 的责任适用于货运代理和船公司，要求其按阿联酋法律满足申报要求，包括按时提交、提供完整信息和确保数据质量。NAIC 仅对不合规行为发出处罚。其他违法行为（如刑事犯罪）移交有关当局。 / NAIC applies to freight forwarders and shipping lines for meeting filing requirements. NAIC only issues penalties for non-compliance. Other legal violations are referred to relevant authorities.
MPCI 申报需要什么文件？
What documents are required for MPCI filing?
船公司：拼箱货物主提单（MBL）或直达货物直接提单。货运代理：拼箱货物分提单（HBL）。 / Shipping Lines: Master Bill of Lading (MBL) for consolidated shipments or Direct Bill of Lading for direct shipments. Freight Forwarders: House Bill of Lading (HBL) for consolidated shipments.
共装场景下需要提交哪份提单？
In co-loading scenarios, which Bill of Lading needs to be filed?
船公司提交其签发的提单。NVOCC 提交其签发的提单。每一层的货运代理提交其签发的提单，最低层货运代理提交包含实际收货人和发货人的提单。 / Shipping lines file the Bill of Lading they issue. NVOCCs file the Bill of Lading they issue. Freight forwarders at each level file the Bill of Lading they issue, with the lowest-level filing the Bill including actual consignee and shipper.
哪些货物类型需要 MPCI 申报？
Which types of cargo require MPCI filing?
MPCI 申报适用于通过阿联酋的集装箱海运货物，包括进口、过境、中转或 FROB（船上剩余货物）。FROB 和中转申报仅由船公司负责。 / MPCI filing is required for containerized maritime cargo that is imported, transiting through, transshipped, or FROB in the UAE. FROB and transshipment filings are only required from shipping lines.
哪些货物免予 MPCI 申报？
What shipments are excluded from MPCI filing?
出口货物、非集装箱货物、木质船舶、空集装箱和船员个人物品。这些豁免可能在未来阶段修订。 / Export shipments, non-containerized cargo, wooden vessels, empty containers, and crew's personal effects. These exclusions may be revised in future phases.
进口、过境、中转和 FROB 货物如何定义？
How does NAIC define import, transit, transshipment, and FROB shipments?
进口：最终交货地点在阿联酋境内的货物。过境：在阿联酋海港卸货，通过陆路或空路运往阿联酋以外最终目的地的货物。中转：在阿联酋一个或多个海港卸货，再装载到另一艘船到达阿联酋以外最终目的地的货物。FROB：到达阿联酋海港但未卸货仍留在船上的货物。 / Import: Final delivery inside UAE. Transit: Discharged at UAE seaport, transported by land/air to destination outside UAE. Transshipment: Unloaded at UAE seaport, reloaded onto another vessel. FROB: Arrives at UAE seaport but remains on board.
货运代理或船公司没有技术能力，有哪些选项？
As a freight forwarder or shipping line without technical capabilities, what are my options for filing with NAIC?
可以通过 NAIC 的认证服务提供商提交。他们提供界面供手动输入或上传文件，并负责将数据转换为正确格式提交。 / You can submit through one of NAIC's certified service providers. They provide an interface for manual entry or document upload, handling conversion and submission in required format.
使用运输管理系统（TMS）的货运代理如何申报？
I am a freight forwarder with a Transport Management System (TMS). How can I file to NAIC?
检查您的 TMS 提供商是否是 NAIC 的认证服务提供商。如果是，现有系统应能无缝处理申报，无需额外数据输入。 / If your TMS provider is one of NAIC's certified service providers, your existing system should handle filing seamlessly, without additional data entry.
提交申报时出现技术故障怎么办？
What happens if there is a technical failure when submitting a filing?
如果申报方系统宕机，必须通知 NAIC，以便暂时豁免申报直到系统恢复。如果 NAIC 系统宕机，申报方将收到通知，申报也将被豁免。 / If filer's system is down, they must notify NAIC so filing can be temporarily waived. If NAIC's system is down, filers will be notified, and filings will be waived for the duration of the outage.
不遵守 MPCI 申报要求会有什么处罚？
What are the penalties for non-compliance with MPCI filing requirements?
不遵守 MPCI 申报要求（包括迟交、缺失或数据错误）将被视为违规，可能导致罚款。 / Failure to comply including late submissions, missing filings, or incorrect data will be considered a violation and may result in fines.

NAIC 响应与转介 | NAIC Response & Referrals

NAIC 可能的响应类型有哪些？
What are the possible response types from NAIC?
ACT（评估完成）：批准装载。ACW（评估完成含警告）：有非关键问题但批准。RFI（信息请求）：需要额外信息。DNL（禁止装载）：不允许装载。PSF（等待子申报）：缺少所需分提单。EXP（申报过期）：因未响应 RFI 200 天内过期。 / ACT: Approved. ACW: Approved with non-critical issues. RFI: Additional details required. DNL: Cannot be loaded. PSF: Missing sub-filings. EXP: Filing expired due to inaction.
NAIC 收到申报后预计响应时间是多少？
What is the expected response time from NAIC after a filing submission?
大多数情况下，NAIC 自动快速处理，提供即时响应。但某些提交可能需要 NAIC 官员手动评估，会延长响应时间。建议尽早提交以减少延迟。 / NAIC processes filings automatically and promptly. However, certain submissions may require manual assessment, extending response time. Submit as early as possible.
如果 NAIC 响应延迟该怎么办？
What should filers do if the NAIC response is delayed?
如果所有请求都延迟响应，可能是技术问题。请联系 NAIC 支持。如果确认是技术问题，申报可能被豁免。如果延迟与特定货物有关，可能是手动评估，建议联系 NAIC 获取澄清。 / If all requests are delayed, it may be a technical issue. Contact NAIC support. If confirmed, filing may be waived. For specific shipment delays, it may be manual assessment; contact NAIC for clarification.
未获得 ACT 批准装载货物会有什么后果？
What are the consequences of loading cargo without ACT approval?
未获 ACT 批准装载货物是违规行为，可能导致罚款和其他执法措施。货物也可能在到达时被拦截。 / Loading without ACT approval is a violation that may result in fines and other enforcement actions. The shipment may also be subject to interception upon arrival.
收到 ACW 转介可以装载货物吗？
Can filers load cargo if they receive an ACW referral?
可以，货物可以装载。但 ACW 表示提交数据有问题，应在后续申报中纠正。 / Yes, cargo can be loaded. However, ACW indicates an issue with the submitted data that should be corrected in future filings.
收到 ACW 转介需要提交更新吗？
Do filers need to submit an update if they receive an ACW referral?
不需要，ACW 不需要更新，因为它不会阻止评估决定。但申报方应解决问题以避免未来类似警告。 / No, ACW does not require an update, as it does not prevent the assessment decision. However, the filer should address the issue to avoid similar warnings.
收到信息请求（RFI）应如何响应？
How should filers respond to a Request for Information (RFI)?
申报方必须通过更新的电子申报提供请求的信息。根据 RFI 的性质，可能涉及更新申报中的必要细节或附加文件（如发票或装箱单）。 / Filers must provide the requested information via an updated filing submission. Depending on the nature, this may involve updating necessary details or attaching additional documents.
RFI 可以通过电子邮件回复吗？
Can an RFI be answered via email?
不可以，RFI 必须通过 NAIC 系统中的更新电子申报来回应。 / No, RFIs must be addressed through updated electronic filing in the NAIC system.
为响应 RFI 进行电子更新时是否需要修改物理提单？
Should filers update the physical Bill of Lading when making electronic updates in response to RFI?
不一定。除非其他当局要求，NAIC 系统中的电子更新不需要修改物理提单。 / Not necessarily. Electronic updates in the NAIC system do not require amendments to the physical Bill of Lading unless mandated by other authorities.
响应 RFI 后可以立即装载货物吗？
Is cargo loading allowed immediately after responding to an RFI?
只有在提交 RFI 响应后获得更新的 ACT 或 ACW 响应后，才允许装载货物。 / Cargo loading is allowed only after receiving an updated ACT or ACW response following the RFI response submission.
收到 DNL 转介该怎么办？
What should happen when a DNL referral is received?
如果收到 DNL（禁止装载），货物不得装载到船舶上。建议在各分提单获得批准后再合并，以避免延迟和额外处理。 / If DNL is received, the shipment must not be loaded. It is recommended to get approvals for individual house bills before consolidation.
PSF（等待子申报）转介是什么意思？
What does a PSF (Pending Sub-Filing) referral mean?
PSF 表示拼箱货物中有一个或多个分提单尚未由责任方提交。例如，当船公司提交拼箱货物的主提单时，货运代理负责分别提交底层分提单。如果缺少任何分提单，船公司将收到 PSF 通知。 / A PSF indicates that one or more HBLs in a consolidated shipment have not been filed by the responsible party.
收到 PSF 转介时船公司应该怎么做？
What should a shipping line do when receiving a PSF referral?
船公司无需采取任何行动，但可以选择跟进货运代理以加速申报。 / The shipping line is not required to take any action, but they may choose to follow up with the freight forwarder to expedite the filing.
什么时候会收到 EXP 转介？
When is an EXP referral received?
当申报方收到 NAIC 的 RFI 但在指南规定的 200 天期限内未响应时，将发出 EXP（申报过期）。收到 EXP 后必须从头重新启动申报流程。 / An EXP is issued when a filer receives an RFI but fails to respond within the 200-day timeframe. The filer must restart the filing process from the beginning.

注册 | Registration

不注册可以直接提交申报吗？
Can a filer submit a filing without registering on the MPCI portal?
不可以，所有申报方必须在提交任何申报前向 NAIC 注册。注册期间分配的 MPCI Party ID 必须包含在所有提交中。 / No, all filers must register with NAIC before submitting any filings. The MPCI Party ID must be included in all submissions.
什么是 MPCI Party ID，如何获取？
What is an MPCI Party ID, and how do I obtain it?
MPCI Party ID 是 NAIC 向注册申报方颁发的唯一标识符。船公司和货运代理必须通过 NAIC 门户网站注册以获取 MPCI Party ID。 / An MPCI Party ID is a unique identifier issued by NAIC to registered filers. Shipping lines and freight forwarders must register via the NAIC portal to obtain their MPCI Party ID.
MPCI Party ID 的用途是什么？
What is the MPCI Party ID used for?
MPCI Party ID 用于每次提交中识别申报方。如果申报方代表另一方提交，必须包含自己的 MPCI Party ID 和所代表方的 MPCI Party ID。对于拼箱货物，拼箱方必须提供主提单方的 MPCI Party ID，主提单方也必须提供拼箱方的 MPCI Party ID。 / The MPCI Party ID is used to identify the filer in every submission. For consolidated shipments, the consolidator must provide the MPCI Party ID of the party filing the MBL, and the master filer must provide the MPCI Party ID of the consolidator.
货运代理如何获取船公司提交主提单的 MPCI Party ID？
I am a freight forwarder. How do I get the MPCI Party ID of the shipping line filing the master bill?
所有船公司的 MPCI Party ID 列表均发布在 NAIC 门户网站上，可以查询所需的 ID。 / A list of all shipping lines' MPCI Party IDs is published on the NAIC portal, where you can look up the required ID.
船公司如何获取货运代理提交分提单的 MPCI Party ID？
I am a shipping line. How do I get the MPCI Party ID of the freight forwarder filing the house bills?
货运代理的 MPCI Party ID 不公开，必须直接从货运代理获取。 / The freight forwarder's MPCI Party ID is not publicly available, so you must obtain it directly from the freight forwarder.
在阿联酋有多个办事处的货运代理或运输代理，每个办事处需要单独的 MPCI Party ID 吗？
I am a freight forwarder or shipping agent with multiple offices in the UAE. Do I need a separate MPCI Party ID for each office?
有两种选择：1) 注册一次，所有办事处使用一个 MPCI Party ID。2) 为每个办事处分别注册，获得单独的 MPCI Party ID。 / You have two options: Register once and use a single MPCI Party ID for all offices, or register separately for each office.
船公司已从公布的列表中获得 MPCI Party ID，还需要在 NAIC 门户网站注册吗？
I am a shipping line and have already obtained my MPCI Party ID from the published list. Do I still need to register on the NAIC portal?
是的，即使已从列表中获得 MPCI Party ID，仍需要完成 NAIC 门户网站上的注册流程。 / Yes, even if you have already obtained your MPCI Party ID from the published list, you are still required to complete the registration process on the NAIC portal.
在阿联酋有多个办事处的船公司，每个办事处需要分别注册吗？
I am a shipping line with multiple offices in the UAE. Do I need to register separately for each office?
不需要，船公司必须注册一次，每个船公司分配一个 MPCI Party ID，所有办事处使用。 / No, shipping lines must register once, and each shipping line is assigned one MPCI Party ID for all offices.
有哪些委托申报选项？
What delegation options are available for filing?
货运代理：可以委托另一本地或海外货运代理，但保留法律合规责任。船公司：可以委托本地或海外运输代理。如果船公司在阿联酋没有法律代表，可以委托运输代理承担申报和法律责任。 / Freight Forwarders: Can delegate to another local or overseas freight forwarder but remain legally liable. Shipping Lines: Can delegate to local or overseas shipping agents. If no legal representation in UAE, can delegate both filing and liability to a local agent.
本地货运代理如何委托另一货运代理代为申报？
I am a local freight forwarder. How do I delegate another freight forwarder to file on my behalf?
注册期间必须启用委托。然后线下向委托的货运代理提供您的 MPCI Party ID。申报时，委托方必须同时提交自己的 MPCI Party ID 和您的 MPCI Party ID。 / During registration, you must enable delegation. Then provide your MPCI Party ID to the delegated freight forwarder offline. When filing, the delegated freight forwarder must submit both their own and your MPCI Party ID.
本地货运代理如何管理哪些货运代理可以代表其申报？
I am a local freight forwarder. How can I manage which freight forwarders can file on my behalf?
可通过 NAIC 门户随时启用或禁用委托：1) 允许所有货运代理代为申报。2) 只允许特定货运代理。3) 完全禁用委托，任何代申报将被拒绝。 / You can enable or disable delegation at any time through the NAIC portal: Enable all, allow only specific, or disable entirely.
运输代理如何获取所代表船公司的 MPCI Party ID？
I am a shipping agent. How do I get the MPCI Party ID of the shipping line I am filing for?
可直接从船公司获取，或访问 NAIC 门户网站查看所有船公司的 MPCI Party ID 列表。 / You can either obtain it directly from the shipping line or access it from the NAIC portal where all shipping lines' MPCI Party IDs are listed.
运输代理代表船公司申报，需要单独注册吗？
I am a shipping agent filing on behalf of a shipping line. Do I need to register separately?
是的，运输代理必须单独注册，并在注册期间指定将代表哪些船公司申报。 / Yes, shipping agents must register separately and specify the shipping lines they will be filing on behalf of during registration.
船公司委托运输代理处理申报，船公司仍需要注册吗？
I am a shipping line delegating a shipping agent to handle the filing. Do I still need to register?
是的，船公司即使委托运输代理申报也必须注册。 / Yes, shipping lines must register, even if they delegate filing to a shipping agent.
运输代理不代表船公司申报，也不作为货运代理，需要注册吗？
I am a shipping agent, but I will not be filing on behalf of a shipping line and I am not acting as a freight forwarder. Do I still need to register?
不需要，如果运输代理不负责作为运输代理或货运代理进行申报，则不需要注册。 / No, if you are not responsible for filing as a shipping agent or freight forwarder, you do not need to register.
本地货运代理将所有申报委托给海外对应方，还需要注册吗？
I am a local freight forwarder, but I will be delegating all filings to my overseas counterparts. Do I still need to register?
是的，本地货运代理必须向 NAIC 注册，即使委托给海外对应方。 / Yes, local freight forwarders must register with NAIC, even if they delegate filing to their overseas counterparts.
海外货运代理未被委托向 NAIC 申报，需要注册吗？
I am an overseas freight forwarder, but I am not delegated to file with NAIC. Do I still need to register?
不需要，海外货运代理不需要注册，除非直接通过 NAIC 申报。 / No, overseas freight forwarders do not need to register unless they are directly filing with NAIC.
同时作为货运代理和运输代理，需要注册两次吗？
I am a freight forwarder who also acts as a shipping agent. Do I need to register twice?
不需要，可以注册一次，对两个角色使用同一个 MPCI Party ID：作为货运代理只需提供自己的 MPCI Party ID；作为运输代理代表船公司申报时，必须同时提供自己的和船公司的 MPCI Party ID。 / No, you can register once and use the same MPCI Party ID for both roles: As a freight forwarder, provide only your own MPCI Party ID. As a shipping agent, provide both your own and the shipping line's MPCI Party ID.

特殊场景 | Special Scenarios & Other Questions

多式联运货物需要申报吗？
Are intermodal shipments required to file?
目前，MPCI 仅适用于海运货物。空运货物申报属于 PLACI 计划。每种运输方式需要分别申报。 / Currently, MPCI filing is required only for maritime shipments. Air cargo filing falls under the PLACI program, and separate filing will be required for each mode of transport.
支线船需要单独申报吗？
Do feeder vessels have to file?
支线船不需要单独申报，除非它们作为第一个到达点进入阿联酋并运营自己的提单。如果货物在单份提单下覆盖整个旅程（包括到达阿联酋前在支线船中转），则应为干线船完成申报，支线船无需额外申报。 / Feeder vessels do not need a separate filing unless they are entering the UAE as the first point of arrival and operating under their own Bill of Lading. If cargo is moving under a single BOL covering the entire journey, the filing should be completed for the main vessel.
如果船舶最初未计划到达阿联酋，但后来更改路线怎么办？
What happens if a vessel was not initially scheduled to arrive in the UAE but later changes its route?
一旦确认船舶将到达阿联酋港口，必须立即提交申报，即使这最初不在计划之内。 / A filing must be submitted as soon as it is confirmed that the vessel will be arriving at a UAE port, even if this was not originally planned.
MPCI 申报有费用吗？
Are there any charges for MPCI filing?
NAIC 不收取申报费用。但第三方服务提供商可能根据其定价模式收费。 / NAIC does not charge fees for filing. However, third-party service providers may apply charges, depending on their pricing model.
