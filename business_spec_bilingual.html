1. Purpose / 目的

The National Advance Information Center or "NAIC", collects travel and trade information from various sources on passenger and cargo movements across the UAE, to perform risk assessment and increase national security levels. NAIC is one of the departments of the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs and Port Security.

NAIC's Pre-Loading Advance Cargo Information (PLACI) program performs risk analysis to identify potential threats from cargo moved through any of the UAE borders. The program has successfully implemented the system for air cargo and this document is to focus on Maritime Pre-Load Cargo Information Program (MPCI) expanding the program scope to cover maritime cargo.

For the MPCI program to be effective in carrying out risk mitigation measures before cargo can be loaded onto vessel bound to the UAE, cargo information is to be filed by either shipping line, NVOCC or freight forwarder within specific time frames and using standard message formats and protocols. NAIC will also respond with assessment response electronically to the filer.

MPCI program represents an additional security layer that virtually extends the UAE borders towards the foreign ports of loading. The program does not interfere nor affect other existing laws or legislations that are imposed and controlled by other agencies.

To facilitate the adoption of the UAE MPCI program, the underlying system design has been prepared and aligned with the principles provided by the UN/EDIFACT standards.
国家预申报信息中心（NAIC）从多种来源收集穿越阿联酋的旅客和货物移动信息，进行风险评估并提升国家安全水平。NAIC 是联邦身份、公民、海关和港口安全局的一个部门。

NAIC 的预装货物信息（PLACI）计划对通过阿联酋任何边境运输的货物进行风险分析，以识别潜在威胁。该计划已成功在空运货物领域实施，本文档聚焦于海运预装货物信息计划（MPCI），将计划范围扩展至海运货物。

为使 MPCI 计划能够在货物装载到驶往阿联酋的船舶之前有效执行风险缓解措施，货物信息须由船公司、NVOCC 或货代在规定时间范围内使用标准消息格式和协议进行申报。NAIC 也将以电子方式向申报方返回评估响应。

MPCI 计划代表了一道额外的安全层，实际上将阿联酋边境延伸到了外国装货港。该计划不干预也不影响其他机构实施和管控的现有法律法规。

为推进阿联酋 MPCI 计划的采纳，底层系统设计已按照 UN/EDIFACT 标准原则进行了准备和对齐。

2. Introduction / 简介

2.1 Overview / 概述

Economic operators such as Shipping Lines or their representatives, Freight forwarders and NVOCCs which are bringing cargo through or into the UAE are required to file shipment data to NAIC. NAIC avails several Technology Service Providers to fulfill such filing requirements.

Economic operators will receive assessment response from NAIC with instructions to either load, do not load or provide additional information for the shipment before loading onto the vessel.

Shipping lines have the option either to integrate through accredited service providers or through direct integration with NAIC system. Filing responsibility and delegation rules for different participants are detailed in subsequent sections.

Throughout the document, "Filer" refers to shipping lines, shipping agents, freight forwarders or NVOCCs, while "Technology Service provider" refers to the technology service providers.
将货物通过或运入阿联酋的经济运营商（如船公司或其代表、货代和 NVOCC）须向 NAIC 申报货物数据。NAIC 提供多家技术服务提供商来满足申报需求。

经济运营商将收到 NAIC 的评估响应，指示在装载到船舶前允许装船、禁止装船或提供额外信息。

船公司可以选择通过认证的服务提供商对接或直接与 NAIC 系统对接。不同参与方的申报责任和委托规则在后续章节详述。

本文件中，"申报方"（Filer）指船公司、船代、货代或 NVOCC；"技术服务提供商"指提供技术服务的一方。

2.2 Legal Framework / 法律框架

The legal framework for establishing NAIC and MPCI program in the UAE is under Federal Decree Law No. 22 of 2018 and Resolution No. 15 of 2019, mandating cargo information filings across all modes of transport.

As per UAE Cabinet Resolution No. (23) of 2023, shipping lines and forwarders must electronically submit Bills of Lading to NAIC before loading at the last foreign departure port outside the UAE. This applies to all containerized maritime cargo destined for import, transshipment, or transit through the UAE.
建立 NAIC 和 MPCI 计划的法律框架基于 2018 年第 22 号联邦法令和 2019 年第 15 号决议，要求在所有运输方式下进行货物信息申报。

根据 2023 年第 23 号阿联酋内阁决议，船公司和货代须在阿联酋境外最后一个外国离港港口装船前，以电子方式向 NAIC 提交提单。此要求适用于所有以进口、转运或过境阿联酋为目的的集装箱海运货物。

3.4 Inclusions / 适用范围

  • Import, Transit and Transshipment (FROB) Shipments: Required within MPCI scope. FROB filings are required only from the shipping line.
  • Containerized maritime cargo: Both FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load) are included.
  • 进口、过境和转运（FROB）货物：在 MPCI 实施范围内。但 FROB 申报仅要求船公司提交。
  • 集装箱海运货物：包括整箱（FCL）和拼箱（LCL）。

3.6 Integration Requirements / 对接要求

Filers generally submit documents through Technology Service Providers, while shipping lines have the option to file through service providers or direct integration with the MPCI system.

Data is transmitted using CUSCAR message based on UN/EDIFACT standards. NAIC responds with:
  • CONTRL message — validation response (acceptance/rejection with error codes)
  • CUSRES message — assessment response (ACT/ACW/RFI/DNL/PSF/EXP)
Message specifications are detailed in the MPCI Message Implementation Guide (MIG).
申报方通常通过技术服务提供商提交文件，船公司可选择通过服务提供商或直接与 MPCI 系统对接。

数据使用基于 UN/EDIFACT 标准的 CUSCAR 消息传输。NAIC 回复：
  • CONTRL 消息 — 验证响应（接受/拒绝及错误代码）
  • CUSRES 消息 — 评估响应（ACT/ACW/RFI/DNL/PSF/EXP）
消息规范详见 MPCI 消息实施指南（MIG）。

4.3.2 Freight Forwarder / Sub Co-loader Filing / 货代/子拼箱方申报

The freight forwarder filing process is similar but simpler:
  1. Submit B/L Message (House B/L)
  2. Message Validation
  3. MPCI Shipment Assessment
  4. Assessment Result → RFI / ACT / ACW / DNL
  5. Sub-Process: System validates availability of all related House B/Ls before responding to shipping line's filing
Note: Filing of House B/Ls may impact Master B/L assessment, and in rare cases, Master B/L may affect House B/Ls assessment.
货代申报流程类似但更简单：
  1. 提交提单消息（分提单 House B/L）
  2. 消息验证
  3. MPCI 货物评估
  4. 评估结果 → RFI / ACT / ACW / DNL
  5. 子流程：系统在回复船公司申报前验证所有关联分提单是否已提交
注意：分提单申报可能影响主提单评估；在极少数情况下，主提单也可能影响分提单评估。

4.6 Delegation Process / 委托流程

4.6.1 Delegation for Freight Forwarders / 货代委托

Freight forwarder delegation options:
  • Local freight forwarders can delegate filing to overseas freight forwarders.
  • Master co-loaders can delegate filing to sub co-loaders.
  • Sub co-loaders can delegate filing to master co-loaders.
During registration, the freight forwarder can:
  • Enable delegation to any other forwarder by default
  • Delegate to specific freight forwarders
  • Disable delegation entirely
The MPCI Party ID must be manually shared with the delegated freight forwarder.
货代委托选项：
  • 本地货代可委托海外货代申报
  • 主拼箱方可委托子拼箱方申报
  • 子拼箱方可委托主拼箱方申报
注册时，货代可以：
  • 默认允许委托给任何其他货代
  • 委托给特定货代
  • 完全禁用委托
MPCI 参与方 ID 须手动分享给被委托的货代。

4.6.3 NAIC Responses in Delegation / 委托情况下的 NAIC 响应

NAIC issues responses primarily to the entity performing the filing (delegated party). The delegating entity can view all filings submitted on their behalf through the NAIC portal. NAIC may also share assessment results with other involved entities to facilitate trade operations.
NAIC 主要向执行申报的实体（被委托方）发送响应。委托方可通过 NAIC 门户查看以其名义提交的所有申报及评估状态。NAIC 也可与其他相关方共享评估结果以促进贸易运营。

5.3 MPCI Party ID / MPCI 参与方 ID

Definition: A unique identifier assigned by NAIC to filers (shipping lines, shipping agents, NVOCCs, freight forwarders). Used for identification, enabling delegation, and linking filings across multiple layers.

Usage Scenarios:
  • All Filings: Filer must provide their MPCI Party ID in each submission
  • Delegation (Local→Overseas FF): Overseas FF must reference local FF's MPCI Party ID
  • Delegation (Shipping Line→Agent): Agent must reference shipping line's MPCI Party ID
  • Consolidation/Co-loading: All parties must include relevant MPCI Party IDs in filing chain
Issuance:
  • FFs and NVOCCs receive ID during onboarding/registration
  • Shipping lines' IDs are publicly available on NAIC portal
  • FF IDs are NOT publicly listed — must be shared internally
定义：NAIC 分配给申报方（船公司、船代、NVOCC、货代）的唯一标识符。用于身份识别、启用委托以及跨层关联申报。

使用场景：
  • 所有申报：申报方须在每次提交中提供自己的 MPCI 参与方 ID
  • 委托（本地→海外货代）：海外货代须引用本地货代的 MPCI 参与方 ID
  • 委托（船公司→船代）：船代须引用船公司的 MPCI 参与方 ID
  • 拼装/共装：所有参与方须在申报链中包含相关方的 MPCI 参与方 ID
发放：
  • 货代和 NVOCC 在注册过程中获得 ID
  • 船公司的 ID 在 NAIC 门户公开可查
  • 货代的 ID 不公开 — 须在相关方之间内部共享

6. Special Business Scenarios / 特殊业务场景

6.1 Consolidation and Co-loading / 拼箱与共装

6.1.1 Overview / 概述

NAIC requires submission of all bills of lading issued throughout the shipping process in consolidation and co-loading scenarios. This includes B/Ls issued by shipping lines and freight forwarders acting as master co-loaders or sub co-loaders.

Example hierarchy:
  • Shipping Line issues BL 1 (Master B/L)
  • NVOCC (Master Co-loader) issues BL 1.1 (House for SL, Master for FF) and BL 1.2 (House for shipper A)
  • Freight Forwarder (Sub Co-loader) issues BL 1.1.1 and BL 1.1.2 (House B/Ls)
NAIC 要求在拼箱和共装场景下提交整个运输过程中签发的所有提单。包括船公司和作为主拼箱方或子拼箱方的货代签发的提单。

层级示例：
  • 船公司签发 BL 1（主提单）
  • NVOCC（主拼箱方）签发 BL 1.1（对船公司是分提单，对货代是主提单）和 BL 1.2（给托运人 A 的分提单）
  • 货代（子拼箱方）签发 BL 1.1.1 和 BL 1.1.2（分提单）

6.1.2-3 Filing Scenarios / 申报场景

Normal Filing:
  • NVOCC files BL 1.1 (consolidated, referencing children BL 1.1.1 & BL 1.1.2) and BL 1.2 (non-consolidation)
  • FF files BL 1.1.1 and BL 1.1.2 (non-consolidation), referencing BL 1.1 as parent
Delegation Options:
  • NVOCC can file on behalf of FF (BL 1.1.1 & BL 1.1.2), specifying FF's MPCI Party ID as issuer
  • FF can file on behalf of NVOCC (BL 1.1), specifying NVOCC's MPCI Party ID as issuer
正常申报：
  • NVOCC 申报 BL 1.1（拼装，引用子提单 BL 1.1.1 和 BL 1.1.2）和 BL 1.2（非拼装）
  • 货代申报 BL 1.1.1 和 BL 1.1.2（非拼装），引用 BL 1.1 为父提单
委托选项：
  • NVOCC 可代货代申报（BL 1.1.1 和 BL 1.1.2），指定货代的 MPCI 参与方 ID 为签发方
  • 货代可代 NVOCC 申报（BL 1.1），指定 NVOCC 的 MPCI 参与方 ID 为签发方

6.1.4 Assessment Response Cascade / 评估响应级联

响应 Response对分提单影响 Impact on House B/L对主提单影响 Impact on Master B/L
ACTHouse ACT 不意味着 Master 也 ACT。Master 须等所有 House 都 ACT。Master ACT 意味着所有底层 House 都已 ACT，可装船。
DNLHouse DNL → 级联到 Master，两者都 DNL。其他 House 不受影响。Master DNL → 级联到所有关联 House，整票禁止装船。
RFIHouse RFI → Master 保持待定，直到所有 House RFI 解决。Master RFI → 不级联到 House。House 可获得 ACT，但 Master 不能装船直到 RFI 解决。
PSF发给船公司/主拼箱方，表示缺少关联分提单申报。应直接联系货代/子拼箱方加快提交。
EXP过期适用于所有层级 — Master 和 House 申报都过期。

6.1.5 Coordination / 协调

Key coordination points:
  • Ensure Consistency: Prevent duplicate filings — only designated party submits each B/L
  • Exchange MPCI Party IDs: Share IDs to accurately specify responsible entities
  • Share B/L Numbers: Communicate B/L numbers to correctly link filings across layers
  • Coordinate on Assessment Results: Align actions based on responses (e.g., DNL → remove affected House and issue new Master)
关键协调要点：
  • 确保一致性：防止重复申报 — 每份提单仅由指定方提交
  • 交换 MPCI 参与方 ID：共享 ID 以准确指定责任实体
  • 共享提单号：沟通提单号以正确关联不同层级的申报
  • 协调评估结果：根据响应对齐行动（如 DNL → 移除受影响的 House 并签发新 Master）

6.2.2 General Considerations / 通用注意事项

  • Updating = modifying existing filing; New B/L = separate filing
  • A cancelled filing cannot be updated — new filing required
  • Cannot have two filings with same B/L number simultaneously (same entity)
  • Filers can only update their own filings
  • No restrictions on which data elements can be updated, but NAIC conducts data quality checks
  • Explanatory notes expected for significant updates
  • 更新 = 修改现有申报；新提单 = 创建独立申报
  • 已取消的申报不能更新 — 须新建申报
  • 同一实体不能同时提交两份相同提单号的申报
  • 申报方只能更新自己的申报
  • 对可更新的数据元素无限制，但 NAIC 会进行数据质量检查
  • 重大更新须提供解释说明

6.2.3 Split Bill of Lading Handling / 拆单处理

A split B/L occurs when part of the cargo under a single B/L is separated and transported on a different vessel (often due to container roll-over).

Shipping Line Options:
  • Leave original B/L unchanged + submit new B/L for separated cargo (referencing original)
  • Update original B/L to reflect split + submit new B/L for separated cargo (referencing updated)
Freight Forwarder:
  • If split affects SL B/L only: Submit new version of House B/L referencing the split SL B/L
  • If split affects both: Leave or update original + submit new forwarder B/L referencing original and split SL B/L
拆单是指单一提单下的部分货物（一个或多个集装箱，或集装箱的一部分）被分离并在不同船舶上运输（通常因集装箱滚装）。

船公司选项：
  • 保持原始提单不变 + 为分离货物提交新提单（引用原始提单）
  • 更新原始提单以反映拆分 + 为分离货物提交新提单（引用更新后的提单）
货代：
  • 如拆分仅影响船公司提单：提交新版分提单引用拆分后的船公司提单
  • 如两者都受影响：保持或更新原始提单 + 提交新货代提单引用原始和拆分后的船公司提单

6.2.4 Assessment Responses for Updates / 更新的评估响应

  • Updating a B/L re-triggers MPCI assessment
  • First version must be submitted before loading; updates can be submitted even after arrival
  • Any update receives one of: DNL, ACT, ACW, RFI, PSF, or EXP
  • To close the filing, final status must be ACT, ACW, or DNL
  • Sequential Updates: If update submitted while previous version under assessment, only latest version receives response
  • DNL post-loading: Shipment should be intercepted upon arrival
  • Filing Expiration: Updates after 200 days from initial filing will be rejected
  • 更新提单将重新触发 MPCI 评估
  • 第一个版本须在装船前提交；更新可在货物到达后提交
  • 每次更新都会收到以下之一：DNL、ACT、ACW、RFI、PSF 或 EXP
  • 要关闭申报，最终状态必须是 ACT、ACW 或 DNL
  • 连续更新：如在前一版本评估期间提交更新，仅最新版本会收到响应
  • 装船后 DNL：货物到达后应被拦截
  • 申报过期：初始申报后 200 天后的更新将被系统拒绝
